About Me

I am currently learning front-end web development through Treehouse.

Previously, I was responsible for conceptualizing and creating a wide range of print and web products that brought Jobs for the Future programs and policy recommendations to stakeholders nationwide.

I maintained JFF’s consistent look and feel, as well as ensuring the appropriate use of the JFF brand across all mediums.

I have also designed branding for Meg Flanagan Education Solutions (formerly MilKids Education Consulting) and have designed websites for Julia Gilstein Editing. I have also designed print products for Cei Builders Inc. I have interned at the New Bedford Whaling Museum, Out of the Box Creative, and Medium Studio. I also served as an assistant librarian at the University of Massachusetts, Dartmouth for four years, where I was responsible for maintaining the library’s Electronic Reserve system for professors.

I graduated Magna Cum Laude from the University of Massachusetts, Dartmouth, with a Bachelor’s in graphic design/letterform and a minor in art history.

Specialties: HTML, CSS, JavaScript along with Adobe InDesign, Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Acrobat Reader, MS Word, MS PowerPoint, and MS Excel (basic knowledge).
