About Me

I am a front-end web developer and graphic designer, with interests in reading, baking, knitting/crocheting, and sewing. Having designed for non-profits, educators, editors, and small businesses I bring my knowledge of design to print and web development creating something truly awesome

Previously, I was a graphic designer for Jobs for the Future where I was responsible for conceptualizing and creating a wide range of print and web products that brought their programs and policy recommendations to stakeholders nationwide. I maintained JFF’s consistent look and feel, as well as ensuring the appropriate use of the JFF brand across all mediums.

Freelancing, I consulted and designed branding for Meg Flanagan Education Solutions (formerly MilKids Education Consulting) and have designed websites for Julia Gilstein Editing. I have also designed print products and truck signage for Cei Builders Inc.

I graduated Magna Cum Laude from the University of Massachusetts, Dartmouth, with a Bachelor’s in visual design/letterform and a minor in art history. I also have my Techdegree certification through Team Treehouse for front-end web development.
